What is Qout?

Qout is a smart fridge conversion kit designed to help households reduce food waste and manage groceries more efficiently. Using camera and AI, Qout detects what goes in and out of the fridge, tracks expiration dates, organizes items into categories, and connects everything to a simple mobile app.

Key Features

Automatic Item Detection

Camera and barcode scanning recognize items instantly.

Smart Inventory

Organizes items into categories like dairy, vegetables, fruits.

Shopping List

Automatically updates a shopping list when items run low.

Expiry Date Alerts

Get notified before items expire so you can use them in time.

Recipe Suggestions

AI suggests recipes based on what’s inside your fridge.

How Qout Works

The Qout Conversion Kit

The kit includes a compact camera module, processing unit, and display for real-time status updates. It is designed to fit into most standard fridges without major modifications.

Our goal is to make installation simple and reversible, so households can upgrade any existing fridge instead of buying an entirely new smart appliance.
